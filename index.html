The Ethics Reviewer Project

For our second year Systems Engineering project we have taken on an idea present to us by Professor Richard Moorhead. The aim of the system is a learning aid for Law students at UCL to work through The Leveson Inquiry, submitting their reasoning and opinions to the lecturer's screen via a web applet client.
